Never Lie

By Freida McFadden — A chilling psychological thriller

About the Book

Newlyweds Tricia and Ethan think they’ve found their dream home in the snowy suburbs—until they uncover the dark past of the house’s previous owner, Dr. Adrienne Hale, a famed psychiatrist who vanished under mysterious circumstances. As snow traps them inside, secrets unravel... and someone might not make it out alive.

About the Author

Freida McFadden is a practicing physician specializing in brain injury. She is also a bestselling author known for her gripping psychological thrillers. With clever twists and suspenseful pacing, her stories explore the dark secrets people hide—and what happens when they come to light.
